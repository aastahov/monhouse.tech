Big Monitoring Meetup

Big Monitoring Meetup is an opportunity to learn about modern monitoring systems for solving specific problems, news and trends of the industry, to communicate with like-minded people, partners and colleagues.

The main directions of the conference:
‣ monitoring systems for various tasks and industries
‣ practical experience in the operation of monitoring systems
‣ infrastructure and business in terms of monitoring
‣ monitoring and management during IoT and IIoT
‣ monitoring and ML / DML / AI - new features
‣ visualization of infrastructure and business processes
‣ information security
